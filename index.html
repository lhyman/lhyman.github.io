Crowdvocate empowers nonprofits to crowdfund and crowdsource advocacy videos for their cause.

WeLobby democratizes political fundraising and lobbying. By collecting the donations of small donors and tying those donations to a specific cause, WeLobby enables these donors to make their voice heard.
